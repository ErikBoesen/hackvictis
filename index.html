A hackathon for high school students.

MasonHacks is a chance for high school students to have fun building new and creative apps, websites, and other software.

FAQ

Q: Can I compete online?

A: Yes! If you're unable to attend the event, you're welcome to submit your projects virtually.


Q: Will there be prizes?

A: Yes! We will be awarding cash prizes of a $500 value to the top three teams.


Q: Can I compete on my own?

A: Sure! If you would prefer to be your own team, that's just fine.


Q: Who runs MasonHacks?

A: The event is run by a partnership between George Mason Robotics Team 1418 and the George Mason Hack Club.

Benefactors

Proceeds from this event will be donated to the following charitable organizations working to do good things in the tech industry.
