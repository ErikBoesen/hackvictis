MasonHacks 2018

May 12th, 2018

  • What: MasonHacks is a hackathon for students in ninth to twelfth grade.
  • Who: Individual members or teams of up to five may participate.
  • Where: The event will be held at George Mason High School. Students unable to travel to Mason will be able to attend and submit their projects virtually from home or their own schools.
  • When: May 12th, 2018, from 10AM to 5PM.
  • Why: To give students an opportunity to have fun, build useful projects, and win prizes of up to [amount]. Proceeds benefit the Electronic Frontier Foundation ()

Benefactors

Proceeds from MasonHacks will be donated to the Electronic Frontier Foundation, which helps to support human rights on the internet.
